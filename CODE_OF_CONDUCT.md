# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard:

- be respectful
- be direct
- assume good intent
- focus discussion on the code, data, and behavior
- do not harass, demean, or intimidate other contributors

Unacceptable behavior includes personal attacks, discrimination, harassment, deliberate disruption, and publication of private information.

Maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributors that violate these expectations.
